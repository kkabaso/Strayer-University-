About Us

Word from CEO of Kennedy Car Dealers of Dallas


Kennedy Kabaso

At Kennedy Car Dealers of Dallas, we proudly serve the greater Dallas, Frisco, Lewisville and McKinney areas through our core values of commitment to people, superior customer service and excellence in product quality. Our employees are exceptionally capable, motivated, and share our principles of integrity and excellence. It is our promise to always provide quality service that exceeds all expectations!


We currently have a huge inventory of New and Used vehicles in-stock. Our new vehicle line-up includes the 4-Runner, Avalon, Camry, Corolla, Highlander, Land Cruiser, Prius, RAV4, Sequoia, Sienna, Tacoma, Tundra, Venza and Yaris. Our goal is to help you find the vehicle that best suits you. If we don't have the vehicle, we will try our best to locate it elsewhere and bring it to you. Once you've selected your perfect new or used car, our Finance Department is ready to help!


Kennedy Car Dealers of Dallas is a family owned and operated establishment which has served the cities of Plano & Dallas for over 2 years. We are active members for the Plano Chamber of Commerce and proud Founding Sponsors of Plano Independent School District Athletic Program. We have an ongoing commitment to our local residents and business owners by providing the highest level customer service.
